About Us

AccuBooks is a leading provider of accounting software designed to help businesses manage their financial processes efficiently and accurately.

Our software offers a wide range of features to facilitate financial management, including the following financial statements:

  • Income Statement: Provides a summary of a company's revenues, expenses, and net income or loss for a specific period.
  • Owner Equity Statement: Shows changes in the owner's equity over a specific time period, including investments, withdrawals, and retained earnings.
  • Balance Sheet: Presents a snapshot of a company's financial position, including its assets, liabilities, and owner's equity, at a specific point in time.
  • T-Accounts: Used to visualize the effects of transactions on specific accounts, showing debits and credits.
  • Trial Balance: Summarizes all the balances of accounts in the general ledger to ensure they are in balance before preparing financial statements.

In addition to these financial statements, AccuBooks also provides comprehensive account management functionalities. You can easily create and manage accounts, track transactions, and make journal entries.

To get started, simply navigate to the following sections in our software:

  • Accounts: Manage your accounts, including creating new accounts and updating existing ones.
  • Create Account: Add a new account to the system, providing all the necessary details.
  • Create Transaction: Record a new transaction, specifying the accounts involved and the corresponding debit and credit amounts.
  • Make Entry: Enter journal entries to record the effects of various transactions on the accounts.
